# ApexOS App: Spotify Connect

Play your Spotify music on your ApexOS device.

![Supports aarch64 Architecture][aarch64-shield] ![Supports amd64 Architecture][amd64-shield]

## About

The Spotify Connect app (formerly known as add-on) allows you to use your
device, running ApexOS, to play your Spotify music. This app uses the
Spotify Connect protocol, which makes it a device that can be controlled
by all the official Spotify clients.

This app is based on the excellent [librespot][librespot] project.
